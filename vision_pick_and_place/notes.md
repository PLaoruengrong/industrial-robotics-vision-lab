## Vision-Guided Pick-and-Place System

Features:

- RGB-based object segmentation
- adaptive thresholding
- blob detection
- homography mapping
- coordinate transformation
- robotic pose generation
- automated pick-and-place execution

Environment:

- MATLAB
- UR5e cobot simulation

Additional custom implementation:

- black-object detection
- lighting robustness optimization
- threshold tuning for varying illumination conditions
